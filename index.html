Music Workshop

An event which will help the Music enthusiast to develop skills to play an instrument

About
MUSIC is the sweetest part of our life.

Some of us want to learn what Music means – its fundamentals, applications, medium and its limits. To produce the melodious Music we need an instrument – may it be Flute, Keyboard, Piano, Guitar, Tabla etc. To produce a melody we need to learn how to play/use these Music instruments. So here is an attempt to help the Music enthusiasts achieve their learning goals with their respective instruments.

Our Goal
An honest attempt to purely share the knowledge

We are looking to organise a Music workshop which will help the Music enthusiast to develop skills to play an instrument. It will be two days event. Music enthusiasts from various background, skill levels (beginners, intermediate and advanced) can join this and benefit from this. Extremely talented, experienced and popular Masters in their respective instruments/areas will guide the participants.


Events

  • Flute
  • Indian Bamboo Flute is considered as a “Devine Instrument”. To help understand the concepts of all three levels of expertise beginners, intermediate and advanced, we will be graced by Three Masters of Flute – Mr. Pravin Gulve, Mr. Radhe and Mr. Jaydip Mavani. . In this event they have kindly agreed to exhibit their Flutes and give us an opportunity to try and buy their Flutes in the event.
  • Guitar and Keyboard
  • None of the Music band is complete without Keyboard and Guitar, they are the heart of Music band. The contemporary Keyboards are so powerful that they can produce any sort of sound. Hence they have been chosen to be part of this workshop.This Music Workshop is kindly sponsored by Mr. Sharan Hiremath, He has been thoroughly trained by Mr. Hamsalekha Sir for years.
